Experiment 2 - Vector Art, Animation & Interactivity

Snail Race

Snail Race involves 2 snails racing along their slime trails created by noise.

Instructions: Use the left and right arrow keys to modify the yellow snail's trail.

Gradienting Circles

Gradienting Circles is a mesmerizing piece in which the random circles slowly change color.

Instructions: Use the keyboard to add spots of random colors or press r,g,b to add those corresponding colors.

Space Debris

Space Debris is a physics simulation, where the debris attracted to the cursor and the planets while repelled by each other.

Instructions: Move your cursor around to move the debris. Click the screen to spawn a new start for all of the objects.
